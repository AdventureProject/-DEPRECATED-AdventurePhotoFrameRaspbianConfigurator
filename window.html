PhotoFrame Configurator

Welcome to the PhotoFrame Configurator, please follow the steps below to setup your PhotoFrame's WiFi connection.

Step 1: Select the USB drive titled CONFG

Please ensure that you have inserted the special Config USB drive that came with your PhotoFrame.

Step 2: Enter your WiFi network's name (SSID)

Step 3: Enter your WiFi network's password (WPA-PSK)

Currently only WPA-PSK network keys are supported.

Step 4: Write Config to USB Config Key

Step 5: Remove Config Key, and insert into PhotoFrame. Wait 5 seconds, then remove Config Key.

Your PhotoFrame should reboot and it should now be on your WiFi network!
